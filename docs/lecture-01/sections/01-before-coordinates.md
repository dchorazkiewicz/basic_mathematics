<section id="before-coordinates" class="section">
  <h2>Before coordinates</h2>
  <p>A Euclidean plane already contains points, straight lines, right angles and lengths. Coordinates do not create these objects. They provide a systematic numerical description after we make several explicit choices.</p>
  <div class="statement">
    <div class="statement-label">Available geometry</div>
    <p>Before coordinates we may construct lines through points, perpendicular lines, circles, intersections and lengths. What is missing is an origin, orientation and numerical scale.</p>
  </div>
</section>
